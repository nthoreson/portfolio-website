My Methodology

The Lean Marketing Operating System

Most teams think they need more leads. But more leads don't always mean more revenue. Sometimes you just need to fix one broken step in your funnel and everything changes. That's where I come in. Over the last 10 years, I've applied a simple, repeatable approach across 150+ businesses. It works whether you're pre-launch or scaling past product-market fit. Whether you're a solo founder or a team of 20. Here's how it works:

01

Metrics First, Always.

I hated math in school. But now? I'm obsessed with the numbers. If you're not tracking the right metrics, you're just guessing. With every client, I map the customer journey and track key metrics from top to bottom. Traffic. Opt-in rates. Click-throughs. Sales calls. Close rates. Churn. Lifetime value. People want more leads. But really they want more revenue. The key to unlocking more revenue is always found in your metrics.

02

Talk to Customers

Before we write a headline, design a funnel, or touch your messaging, talk to your customers. You might think you know what problem you solve. But what matters is what your customers say. Talk with your customers to uncover how they describe their pain, what pushed them to buy, and what nearly stopped them. This gives us the language we need to craft content that actually converts. It also kills assumptions, fast.

03

Build the Funnel

Now that we know the pain points and see where users are dropping off, we build the system. We start with one clear lead generation strategy based on where your audience already spends time. From there, we create a funnel that removes friction and keeps momentum. Every piece of content, every step in the funnel, and every offer should do one thing: solve the core problem your customers are trying to fix.

04

Track, Test, Adjust

This part gets skipped way too often. But it's where real growth comes from. We track what matters. We test new approaches. And we use those insights to make better decisions. Customer behavior changes. Markets evolve. Messaging that worked a year ago might fall flat today. I help you build that feedback loop into your operations, so you're never relying on guesswork.

05

Scale On Autopilot

Once the system's working, we scale it. This is where automation and AI come in. Not as shiny add-ons, but as tools to help your team move faster and do more with less. We automate what's repeatable, speed up what's slow, and use AI to extend your output without adding overhead. But we don't scale until we know it works.

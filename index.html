§ 04: Methodology

The Lean Marketing
Operating System.

Most teams chase more leads when what they actually need is to fix one broken step in their funnel. Over the last 10 years I've applied one simple, repeatable approach across 150+ businesses, whether pre-launch or scaling past product-market fit. Here's how it works.

01
Diagnose

Metrics first, always.

I used to hate math, and now I'm obsessed with the numbers. If you're not tracking the right metrics, you're just guessing. With every client I map the customer journey top to bottom: traffic, opt-in rates, click-throughs, sales calls, close rates, churn, and lifetime value. People think they want more leads when they really want more revenue, and the key to revenue is always hiding in the metrics.

02
Listen

Talk to customers.

Before we write a headline, design a funnel, or touch your messaging, we talk to your customers. You might think you know the problem you solve, but what matters is what they say. We uncover how they describe their pain, what pushed them to buy, and what nearly stopped them. That gives us the language that actually converts, and it kills assumptions fast.

03
Build

Build the funnel.

Now that we know the pain points and where users drop off, we build the system. We start with one clear lead generation strategy based on where your audience already spends time, then create a funnel that removes friction and keeps momentum. Every piece of content and every offer does one thing: solve the core problem your customers are trying to fix.

04
Refine

Track, test, adjust.

This is where real growth comes from, and it gets skipped far too often. We track what matters, test new approaches, and use those insights to make better decisions. Customer behavior changes and markets evolve, and messaging that worked a year ago can fall flat today. I build that feedback loop into your operations so you're never relying on guesswork.

05
Scale

Scale on autopilot.

Once the system works, we scale it. This is where automation and AI come in, as tools to help your team move faster and do more with less. We automate what's repeatable, speed up what's slow, and use AI to extend your output without adding overhead. We only scale once we know it works.

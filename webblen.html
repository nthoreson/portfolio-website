Webblen

As CMO and co-founder at Webblen, a B2B2C web3 startup that rewards users for attending events using crypto. I successfully built a community around the highly technical web3 mobile application. As a one-person marketing department, I was responsible for all of Webblen's product marketing, including website and graphic design, public relations, brand strategy, and content management. In doing so, I drove 30,000+ users to download the Webblen mobile app, grew the WBLN token to $300,000+ in liquidity, and amassed an online community of 50,000+ individuals, all with zero marketing spend.

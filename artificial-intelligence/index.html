02 / Data Analysis

Turning Client Data into Renewal Revenue

At Lean Marketing, clients were coming up for renewal without a clear way to see the value they'd built. I built an AI system that pulled every piece of engagement data per client — coaching call transcripts, health scores, milestone wins, support interactions, session attendance, and sentiment signals — and synthesized it into a single report. We called it holding up the mirror. Advisors walked into renewals with evidence instead of instinct.

Result → Renewal rate climbed from 19% to 70% after deploying AI-powered engagement reports for 80+ clients.

03 / Content Creation

Calls In, Content Out

The best marketing content for a business is usually buried inside it. I built a content pipeline at Lean Marketing that starts with client coaching calls and ends with finished drafts. Recordings get transcribed and run through a prompt pipeline that extracts key insights, client wins, and teachable moments. Those outputs route into LinkedIn posts, email sequences, and blog drafts — all written in a trained voice built from real content examples.

Example → A 60-minute coaching call turns into 3–5 pieces of finished content in under 20 minutes — grounded in real conversations instead of starting from a blank page.

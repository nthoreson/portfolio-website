AI Systems Built to Move the Number

I've built 20+ AI tools for marketing teams, systems that ship, run in production, and hold up under real client load. Every project here was built to move a specific number, not to demo well in a deck.

The Work

Four systems. Four numbers.

Product Creation

Most AI work gets stuck in prototype mode, an interesting experiment that never becomes a tool anyone opens twice. The 1PMP Architect is a multi-step AI agent that generates a full marketing plan from raw client inputs in under 15 minutes, used by 100+ founders inside the Lean Marketing Accelerator to replace strategy work that used to take days. Lean Intelligence gives advisors a live view across 80+ client accounts without digging through five separate tools.

Data Analysis

Clients were coming up for renewal without a clear way to see the value they'd built, so advisors went in on instinct. I built a system that pulls every piece of engagement data per client, call transcripts, health scores, milestones, support history, and synthesizes it into one report per account. Advisors walk into renewals with the data instead of the pitch. Renewal rate climbed from 19% to 70%.

Content Creation

The best marketing content for a business is usually buried inside it, in client calls and advisor insights nobody writes down. I built a pipeline that starts with coaching call recordings and ends with finished drafts, transcribed, run through a model trained on real content examples, and routed into LinkedIn posts, email sequences, and blog drafts. A 60-minute call becomes 3-5 finished pieces in under 20 minutes.

Automation

Most marketing teams have their data spread across five tools with nothing keeping any of it in sync. I built the automations that made one source of truth for the whole team: new client onboards, coaching notes, milestones, and health scores flow into the right places automatically. The team stopped chasing spreadsheets and got back 15+ hours a week.

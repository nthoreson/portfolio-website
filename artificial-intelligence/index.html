The Problem

Most AI investment produces nothing.

A 2025 MIT study on enterprise AI adoption found that 95% of companies investing in generative AI saw no return. The technology wasn't the problem. Researchers traced the failure back to how the projects got built. Teams built their own tools instead of buying proven ones, then pointed them at departments like marketing and sales instead of the back-office workflows where AI reduces cost most reliably.

Every system on this page started from the opposite instinct. Find the one number that's stuck, then build the smallest system that moves it. If it doesn't move that number, it doesn't ship.

Source: MIT Project NANDA, "The GenAI Divide: State of AI in Business 2025."

My Approach

Four rules. No exceptions.

Diagnose First

I don't open a tool until I know exactly what's broken. A project that starts with "let's use AI for this" already has it backward. Start with the constraint. AI is just the fastest way to remove it.

Build for Daily Use

The bar for shipping is whether the team notices if it disappears tomorrow. If a system isn't opened every day, it isn't finished.

Design for Autonomy

Most "AI tools" are a chat window bolted onto a prompt. I build agents that act inside production systems, with enough context to make a decision without someone reviewing every output.

Measure the Outcome

Every system on this page was built against one number. If that number didn't move, the project isn't done, no matter how good the demo looked.

Automation 01

One call in. Four systems updated automatically.

I built the call intelligence automation running underneath Lean Marketing's coaching program. Calls used to end the same way, as a good conversation that lived only in someone's memory. Now every call feeds four systems at once, without anyone touching a keyboard.

01

Transcribe & Analyze

Every coaching call is transcribed and analyzed to surface wins, blockers, and coaching quality in one pass.

02

Client Win Logged

Verified wins get written into the internal system automatically, building the evidence library instead of losing it to memory.

03

CS Handoff

Anything relevant to retention or account health routes straight to the client success team, no manual recap required.

04

Weekly Manager Report

Coaches and managers get a standing report on call quality and coaching patterns, built from every call instead of spot checks.

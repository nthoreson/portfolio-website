Moore Holding Company

212% Traffic Growth in 12 Months

At Moore Holding (formerly Vista), I managed digital strategy and website operations across three portfolio companies. By rebuilding their SEO infrastructure, redesigning key landing pages, and building out email automation, we drove a 212% increase in organic website traffic in under a year — turning their site from a brochure into a lead generation engine.

Motion Corp

Full Brand Build for a Publicly Traded Company

Motion Corp is a publicly traded digital media company that came to me needing both a brand and a web presence built from the ground up. I designed the full visual identity — logo, color system, typography — and built their Webflow website. Every design decision was made with their investors and enterprise partners in mind.

Lean Marketing

Multiple Platforms, One Cohesive System

At Lean Marketing, I've developed and manage multiple online platforms including the main Lean Marketing website, Accelerator program pages, and internal tools used by 100+ clients. DropChain

Brand and Website Built from Zero

When I joined DropChain, there was no brand and no website. I built both from scratch — designing the full visual identity, building out the Webflow site, and creating all the marketing collateral needed to launch a web3 SaaS platform. The brand went on to support a token launch, investor pitches, and a product suite used across the web3 ecosystem.

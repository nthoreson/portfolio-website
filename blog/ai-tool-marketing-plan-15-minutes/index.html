Why Most Marketing Plans Never Get Used

The issue isn't that founders lack information. Most of them have read The 1-Page Marketing Plan, watched the YouTube videos, and attended the workshops.

The issue is that sitting down and building a plan from scratch is harder than it sounds. The blank page is intimidating, the questions feel abstract, and most founders get stuck in the research and rewriting loop, tweaking their positioning for the third time instead of shipping anything.

Six months later, the plan is still unfinished and the business is still running on instinct and hope.

I've watched this happen in real time across every industry I've worked in. It doesn't matter how smart the founder is. The constraint isn't intelligence, it's the gap between knowing what to do and actually doing it.

What I Built

The 1PMP Architect is an AI-powered tool that generates a complete, customized 1-Page Marketing Plan for your business in 15 minutes.

It's built on Allan Dib's 9-block framework from The 1-Page Marketing Plan and Lean Marketing, the same methodology we use with every founder in the Lean Marketing Accelerator. The framework has been validated across more than a million users worldwide.

The tool pulls real inputs from your business (form responses, customer interview notes, CRM data, anything you have) then uses structured AI agents to produce a plan tailored to your actual offer, your audience, and your growth stage.

What you get at the end is not a template to fill in. You get a finished plan you can open, read, and start acting on today.

What's Inside the Plan

The output follows Allan's proven 9-block structure, with every section built specifically for your business:

Target Market. Who you're actually trying to reach, defined with enough specificity that your marketing decisions become obvious.

Messaging. The core message that speaks directly to your target customer's pain, framed in language they actually use.

Media Strategy. The channels where your audience already spends time, prioritized by where you can realistically show up consistently.

Lead Capture. A clear system for turning attention into contact information you can follow up on.

Lead Nurture. What happens after someone raises their hand, and how you move them from curious to ready to buy.

Sales Conversion. The specific mechanism that turns a qualified lead into a paying customer.

World-Class Experience. How you deliver the product or service in a way that creates genuine loyalty.

Increasing Lifetime Value. The playbook for earning more from the customers you already have.

Orchestrating Referrals. The system that turns happy customers into a predictable source of new business.

Every block is populated with your business context, not generic filler.
